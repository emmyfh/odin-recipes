Pita Sandwhich

Description

This is a recipe for an incredibly tasty and nutritious pita breakfast sandwhich

This simple recipe is easy to make and will leave you satisified and ready to face the day!

Ingredients

  • Pita pocket
  • Two large eggs
  • Feta cheese
  • Chopped olives
  • Handful of spinach

Instructions

  1. Scramble the two large eggs
  2. Stir in feta cheese, olives, and spinach
  3. Cut pita pocket in half
  4. Stuff pita with your delicious briny cheesy eggs
  5. Enjoy!
