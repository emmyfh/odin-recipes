Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is a timeless classic most people are familiar with

Using just 3 basic ingredients you can easily make a sandwhich fit for a king

Fun Fact! The PB&J was originally invented in America at a culinary acadmey in Baltimore MD

Ingredients

  • peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • Jelly (grape or strawberry work best)
  • 2 slices of your favorite bread

Instructions

  1. Apply peanut butter to one slice of bread
  2. Apply generously
  3. Spread jelly on other slice
  4. Combine 2 slices with peanutbutter and jelly sides facing in
  5. Cut diagonally (this provides a better eating experience)
  6. Enjoy!
