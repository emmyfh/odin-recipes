Classic Tuna Melt

The Tuna melt is a very easy to make sandwhich served with toasted bread, creamy melted cheese, and of course, tuna!

If you're a fan of tuna and cheese then this might be the perfect sandwhich for you

Ingredients

  • shredded tuna
  • Jalapenos
  • paprika
  • mayo
  • Fontina cheese
  • Whole grain bread

Instructions

  1. dice jalapenos (careful not to use too many!)
  2. Mix tuna with dash of paprika, mayo, and jalapenos
  3. Slice fontina and lay slices on bread
  4. toast bread until cheese is slightly melted
  5. spread tuna mix onto one side of sandwhich and place other slice on top
  6. toast a little longer until bread is golden brown
  7. Cut in half
  8. Enjoy!
